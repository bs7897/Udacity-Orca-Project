Orca Whale

A Orca Whale is also commonly known as the Killer Whale, they travel in groups called pods.

  • Scientific Name: Orcinus Orca
  • Average Lenght: 25 ft.
  • Average Lifespan: 35 years
  • Habitat: Coastal Waters

The Killer Whale belongs to the oceanic dolphin family, of which it is the largest member. Killer whales have a diverse diet, and each individual pods often specialize in particular types of prey. They have been known to prey on other whales and sharks, including the Great White shark.
